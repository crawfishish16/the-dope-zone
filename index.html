Welcome to the Dope Zone.
A state of mind where you are completely unfuckable with.
The Dope Zone is a state of mind where you are un-fuck-able with. Nothing can ruin your perspective on the fact that everything in your life is dope at that moment in time. Regardless of whatever external scenario takes place, you can't help but maintain a positive perspective and see the bright side of a situation or a solution.
We all know what this feels like and have been there before, and we all wish that we could experience that perspective permanently and it would never end.
The Dope Zone doesn't just happen by accident. It is a result of a sequence of events and your subconscious emotional and physical reactions to those events — based on the chemicals that your body and mind excrete.
These chemicals being released are a result of the conditioning that you have experienced in the past from the same or similar scenarios in your life before.

Perfect Schedule Guide
Your complete instructions for building your perfect energetic rhythm.
We are beginning the journey to define what perfect energetic rhythm looks like to you. Deepen the understanding of how an activity over a certain period of time affects you energetically and emotionally.
To remain in your Dope Zone.
What is the Dope Zone?
The Dope Zone is a state of mind where everything is dope. We have all been in a state of mind so perfect and balanced that not even the worst situation can ruin our mood.
You've slept 8 hours, ate healthy, and exercised. Your mind is clear. You are present, relaxed, content, and happy.
We use a sliding + and − scale. For most, the Dope Zone's range is +2 on the high end and −2 on the low. Our objective is to stay within the Dope Zone as much as possible.

The 16 Hour Fundamental
16
Hours awake each day
If you get 8 hours of sleep per night, you have 16 hours of being awake. Your Perfect Day activities must total 16 hours.
Crafting your Perfect Day without Work
Step 1
Establish an unrealistic state of mind — you have no responsibilities.
Step 2
Identify the activities that fill you up emotionally, spiritually, mentally, physically, and energetically.
Step 3
Clearly define the perfect amount of time spent on each activity.
Step 4
Order these activities in the order that you believe is your definition of the perfect energetic order.
Disperse into as many blocks as you like. Get creative.
Step 5
Adhere to the 16 Hour Fundamental — ensure your collective activity hours total 16.

Part 2
Perfect Day with Work
Blend your dream life and current life together.
Step 1
Take a step back into the present moment, while maintaining that things can be whatever way you would like them to be.
Step 2
Identify the 5–7 major work activities you perform on a weekly basis.
Examples: Sales, Marketing, Management, Finances, Admin, Customer service
Step 3
Define the actual time spent on each work activity per day. Rate each with a Dope Zone score.
Key insight
The goal is counterintuitive — we are fitting work INTO our personal life. Not the other way around.
Step 4
Shorten or group personal activities to make room for work, keeping as many as possible.
Step 5
Adhere to the 16 Hour Fundamental — combined schedule must total 16 hours.
